About Food Time

Serving fresh, delicious food with passion since 2018

Our Story

Food Time was born from a simple idea: to create a place where people can enjoy amazing food, made with the freshest ingredients, in a welcoming atmosphere. Founded by Chef Michael Rodriguez in 2018, our restaurant quickly became a favorite spot for food lovers.

What started as a small caf√© with just five tables has grown into a beloved dining destination, but our core values remain the same - quality, passion, and hospitality.

Our Mission

At Food Time, we believe that good food brings people together. Our mission is to create memorable dining experiences through exceptional food, attentive service, and a warm atmosphere. We're committed to sourcing sustainable ingredients, supporting local farmers, and minimizing our environmental impact.
